Lodging

The Mystery Hunt is an MIT event and is best experienced in person at the Institute. For those who are coming from out of town, we have arranged for discounted room blocks at several local hotels.

If you encounter any problems booking rooms, or the room block appears to be full, please get in touch with us and we will work with the hotel on your behalf. (Per our contracts with the hotels, requests for additional rooms need to be submitted through us.)

Hyatt Regency Cambridge

575 Memorial Drive • 617.492.1234 • hyatt.com

Rate: $109 per night (double occupancy, +$25 for each additional person up to four). Book here.

Parking: $20 per 24 hours (self-parking) or $55 per 24 hours (valet). Both include in-and-out privileges.

Breakfast buffet: $21.95 per person with a discount voucher offered at check-in.

Boston Marriott Cambridge (Kendall Square)

50 Broadway • 617.494.6600 • marriott.com

Rate: $109 per night (double occupancy, no extra charge for each additional person up to four). Book here.

Parking: $42 per 24 hours (self-parking) or $47 per 24 hours (valet). Valet parking (but not self-parking) includes in-and-out privileges.

Breakfast buffet: $26.25 per person.

Le Méridien Boston Cambridge (University Park)

20 Sidney Street • 617.577.0200 • marriott.com

Rate: $109 per night (double occupancy, +$10 for each additional person up to four). Book here.

Parking: $35 per 24 hours (self-parking) or $40 per 24 hours (valet). Both include in-and-out privileges.

Breakfast buffet: $27.50 per person.

Royal Sonesta Boston (East Cambridge)

40 Edwin Land Boulevard • 617.806.4200 • sonesta.com

Rate: $109 per night (double occupancy, +$25 for each additional person up to four). Book here.

Parking: $20 per 24 hours (self-parking or valet). Both include in-and-out privileges.

Breakfast buffet: $21.00 per person at the Art Bar.

The Kendall Hotel

350 Main Street • 617.577.1300 • kendallhotel.com

Discount: A Mystery Hunt group discount rate is available by calling the hotel directly at 617.577.1300 between 8:00 am and 5:00 pm and asking for in-house reservations or by using the online promo code MYS9 at kendallhotel.com.

Parking: $20 per night (self-parking). Includes in-and-out privileges, but the garage is first-come-first-served and for current guests only. Cars must vacate the garage by 4:00 pm on the day of checkout.

Breakfast buffet: Complimentary for guests. There is also a complimentary nightly wine hour for guests.
